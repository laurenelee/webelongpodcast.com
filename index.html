Lessons from Unconventional Paths to Tech
About the Pod

A weekly podcast hosted by Lauren Lee that aims to interview career changers & folks that are diversifying the tech industry.

Celebrating Unique Journeys

Sharing stories about the different paths people have taken to enter the tech industry and chatting about the skills they learned in their prior jobs, schooling, or life experiences & how they apply them to their current roles in tech.

Building Community

Inviting those who are curious about breaking into tech to ask questions and learn from diverse leaders in our community.
